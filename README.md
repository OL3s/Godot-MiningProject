# Deep Miner

## High Concept

A 2D mining and expedition game where players deploy to increasingly dangerous planets in search of valuable resources, rare materials, and alien artifacts.

Players must balance risk, battery life, cargo space, defenses, and technology progression while exploring deeper underground and expanding their operation.

The game is designed primarily for solo play and local co-op on a shared screen.

---

# Core Gameplay Loop

1. Select a planet.
2. Pay deployment cost.
3. Land at a temporary base.
4. Mine resources.
5. Explore deeper underground.
6. Avoid hazards and enemies.
7. Return resources to base.
8. Extract safely.
9. Sell resources.
10. Purchase upgrades.
11. Unlock new planets.
12. Repeat.

---

# Core Design Pillars

## Risk vs Reward

The deeper the player travels:

- Better resources
- More valuable artifacts
- Greater danger
- Higher chance of losing the run

## Technology Progression

Players cannot simply farm starter planets forever.

New planets contain materials required to build equipment capable of mining the next tier of resources.

## Player Choice

Players choose:

- What upgrades to buy
- How deep to explore
- Which planet to visit
- How much risk to take
- Whether to focus on mining, combat, automation, or exploration
